Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2024

1. Introduction

At Delhi Chicken Brothers, we strive to provide the best dining experience for our customers. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the conditions under which orders can be cancelled and refunds processed. Please read this policy carefully before placing your order.

2. Order Cancellation

2.1 Cancellation Timeframe

Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placement, provided food preparation has not yet begun. Once our kitchen starts preparing your order, cancellation may not be possible.

2.2 How to Cancel

To cancel your order:

  • Call us immediately at 9211022131
  • Use the cancellation option in your order confirmation email
  • Contact us through our website or mobile app
